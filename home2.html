🧼 Hygiene & Safety Standards

We maintain the highest international hygiene protocols to ensure every tattoo is safe, sterile, and professionally executed.

🧪

Sterilization Process

All tools are sterilized using autoclave systems and medical-grade disinfectants. We follow strict single-use policies for needles.

⚙️

Premium Equipment

We use high-end, industry-certified tattoo machines, ink, and disposable supplies to ensure precision and zero contamination risk.

🛡️

Safety Protocols

Artists wear gloves, masks, and follow WHO-standard hygiene guidelines to maintain a completely safe environment.

Certified Artists

Clean Environment
